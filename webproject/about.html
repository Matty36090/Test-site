About The Blue Suede Shoe

The Blue Suede Shoe is a student project that was created in 2024. The project was created by myself to learn more about web development and to showcase my skills. The project is a website that is dedicated to the history of Elvis Presley and the impact he had on music.
The website features information about the history of ELvis, some of the tracks that have been released, and his family life. The website also features rare images of the king and his family. The website is a work in progress and will be updated regularly with new information and images. Thank you for visiting The Blue Suede Shoe. I hope you enjoy your visit.
If you have any suggestions or feedback, please feel free to contact me.
